Must Have Coworking Tech for 2019

While the Coworking industry continues to expand and evolve, it’s important to adapt the technology in your space accordingly. In our most recent blog, we highlight the critical tech components that Coworking spaces need to implement to thrive in the coming year.

Here are some of the highlights:

A CRM will streamline lead entry, customer lifecycle tracking, vendor relationship management, and increase your efficiency and productivity.
Automated billing is a necessity. Consider the time you’re spending each month in balancing and reconciling your books, plus the outsourced cost of a bookeeper or accountant.
Your space is falling behind in the competitive Coworking market if it doesn’t have the most optimized Wi-Fi. Maximize your connectivity to increase the production of your members.
Facilitate self-service and team / member communication in the member portal of your Coworking software.
